Card Index

  • 0 The Fool: The traveller of the path.
  • I The Magician: Revelation of life eternal.
  • II The High Priestess: The Mysteries
  • III The Empress: Embrace hope.
  • IV The Emperor: Self-discipline.
  • V The Hierophant: Seek knowledge.
  • VI The Lovers: Wed yourself to the earthly or the divine?
  • VII The Chariot: Balance of one's desires.
  • VIII Strength: Revelation of the ability to master oneself.
  • IX The Hermit: A higher state of consciousness.
  • X Wheel of Fortune: Escaping the cycle.
  • XI Justice: Discernment of higher things.
  • XII The Hanged Man: Betraying convention.
  • XIII Death: Move forward or hung on to the past?
  • XIV Temperance: Balance of the conscious and subconscious selves.
  • XV The Devil: Revelation of the worldly trap / consequence of the Lovers.
  • XVI The Tower: Destroying dogma / consequence of the Hanged Man.
  • XVII The Star: Calling forth the soul.
  • XVIII The Moon: The journey.
  • XIX The Sun: Awakening the soul.
  • XX Judgment: Reunite or remain?
  • XXI The World: The triumph of the soul.
